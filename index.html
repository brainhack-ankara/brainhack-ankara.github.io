Information

This is the very first edition of Brainhack Ankara! Our event is going to run completely virtual this year. Brainhack Ankara is local chapter of an umbrella organization Brainhack and its virtual version Brainhack Global. The purpose of Brainhack is to bridge the data science and neuroscience research communities to advance the progress of brain science research. Brainhack is a unique conference that convenes researchers from across the globe and myriad disciplines to work together on innovative projects related to neuroscience.

Brainhack Global is dedicated to a harassment-free conference experience for everyone. Our anti-harassment policy can be found here: Code of Conduct
